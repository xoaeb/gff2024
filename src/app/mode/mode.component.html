Choose an Option to Complete Your In-Car Payment

Proceed with your in-car payment by picking one of the options below for a smooth and convenient transaction.

Fast Tag

Fast-tag enables seamless toll payments as you drive through highways, automatically deducting charges from your prepaid account, ensuring a hassle-free journey.

Drive Through

Experience quick and contactless transactions at drive-thrus with QR payment, making your order process faster and safer with a simple scan.

Fuel

Fuel up effortlessly by scanning a QR code at the pump, ensuring a quick, contactless, and secure payment experience without leaving your vehicle.
